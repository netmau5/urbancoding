    Our Values

    What we're about isn't software or interfaces, we're about creating happiness. Happiness is born from simple, delightful, and useful experiences. Any product that can be these three things is magical and has the power to make people's life better. That's what we believe.

    Our customers care about getting every detail right. Applications should be easy to understand and full of helping hands to keep users on the right path to a job well done. It's easy to keep adding, but it takes discipline to trim the cruft. We work best with people who care about solving a problem simply rather than populating a bullet list of features.

    We're here for the long run and want to be companions to our customers, both in our own products and for those of our clients. Jobs are for paychecks; relationships are for teams. We want to be part of yours.

    How We Work

    Our belief in simplicity isn't just part of what we build but how we build it. Every project is handled by a small team and we ask that you have one too. Big groups often fail to make decisions or act nimbly.

    We treat every relationship like a partnership instead of a requirements black box. Our work is developed in short, concrete iterations to quickly react to ever-evolving nature of software. Each step of the way, we strive to clearly communicate where we're at and what we've done to maximize every opportunity to nail your vision.

    We constantly question our previous decisions and challenge ourselves to find a better way. After every project, we review our work internally and with our clients to ensure that every time you work with us, it will be the best experience you've ever had. Building a long-term relationship with each client is far more important to us than a hurried job that results in mediocrity.
